# Analysis: Stuck at 280W FTP Despite Consistent Threshold Work

## Primary Diagnostic Issue: FTP Stagnation Signal

### The Core Problem
You're at 280W FTP for 6 months with consistent threshold training, yet no progress. This pattern has a specific interpretation:

**TTE stagnation at 60-75 minutes signals need for VO2max work, not more threshold training.** [E] (ec-master-reference / TMT-60)

### Why This Matters
- FTP is the *floor* of your aerobic capacity, not the ceiling
- Your VO2max (mL/kg/min) sets the actual ceiling for sustainable power
- Threshold training (utilization training) maximizes your *current* aerobic capacity; it cannot raise a higher ceiling [R] (training-for-the-uphill-athlete-capacity-vs-utilization-trai)
- VO2max "raises the roof" for FTP to grow into [E] (ec-master-reference / TMT-60)

## Specific Action Items

### 1. **Introduce a VO2max Block**

**Trigger:** FTP unchanged after threshold block + rest + retest [E] (ec-master-reference / TMT-60)

**Protocol Options:**

#### **Classic Long Intervals** (Build phase, ample volume base)
- 4-5 x 4-5 min @ 105-120% FTP
- 3-5 min easy recovery
- Total VO2max time: ~20 min
- Key advantage: Maximizes cardiac strain, peak HR [E] (WD-55, TMT-45)

#### **Ronnestad 30/15** (More novel stimulus)
- 3 sets x 13 reps: 30s @ 94% MAP / 15s @ 77% MAP
- 3 min between sets
- 4.7% 20-min TT power improvement in 3 weeks [R] (Ronnestad 2020)
- **Caveat:** No significant VO2max group difference (p = 0.49); gains likely from W' improvement, not pure VO2max [R] (WD-55)

#### **Micro-burst 40/20** (Time-crunched athletes)
- 3 x 10 min sets of 40s on / 20s off
- High muscle activation, accumulates more time at high absolute power [E] (Bobo, TP)
- Ideal if you have limited weekly training hours

#### **Decreasing Intervals** (Ultra-endurance focus)
- 3:00/2:00/1:00/0:45/0:30 (high) with proportional rest
- Best of long + short: quick VO2max attainment + extended time >90% [R] (Vaccari 2020)

### 2. **Progression Model: Short-to-Long (STL)**

Once you introduce VO2max work:

1. **Accumulation phase**: Build aerobic base with tempo, sweet spot
2. **Transmutation phase**: Introduce VO2max intervals starting with longer durations (~8 min)
3. **Focused VO2max block**: Concentrate on 2-3 week intensive phase
4. **Restoration**: Recovery week before returning to focused block
