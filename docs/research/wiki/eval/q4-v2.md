# 3-Week VO2max Block -- 10 Hours/Week

## Block Structure

Use a **2:1 mesocycle**: 2 weeks loading, 1 week recovery. VO2max blocks require rest every 2-3 weeks (EC TMT-55). Limit high-intensity sessions to 2 per week -- evidence shows no added benefit beyond that (Seiler 2010, Hawley & Bishop 2021).

## Weekly Template

| Day | Session | Detail |
|-----|---------|--------|
| Mon | Rest | Off the bike |
| Tue | **VO2max intervals** | See progression below; 75-90 min total with warm-up/cool-down |
| Wed | Endurance | 90 min @ <65% FTP (IF 0.50-0.65). Optional: 3-5 x 10s sprints early |
| Thu | **Threshold** | Progression: Wk1 3x12 min @ FTP / 5 min rest; Wk2 2x20 min @ FTP / 5 min rest |
| Fri | Rest or easy spin | 30-45 min @ <60% FTP |
| Sat | **VO2max intervals** | Second VO2max session; same protocol as Tue |
| Sun | Long endurance | 2.5-3 hr @ <65% FTP (IF 0.50-0.65) |

**Weekly totals:** ~10 hours. Two VO2max sessions, one threshold session, two endurance rides, one optional recovery spin.

## VO2max Interval Progression

Use the **classic long-interval format** -- simple, well-studied, and ideal for athletes building into VO2max work. Target **105-120% FTP**, RPE 9-9.5/10, cadence 100-120 RPM to reduce muscular fatigue (EC WD-55).

| Week | VO2max Session | Total Time at Intensity | Rest Between Reps |
|------|---------------|------------------------|-------------------|
| 1 | 4 x 3 min @ 110% FTP | 12 min | 3 min easy |
| 2 | 5 x 3.5 min @ 110% FTP | 17.5 min | 3 min easy |
| 3 (recovery) | 3 x 2 min @ 105% FTP | 6 min | 3 min easy |

Follow the **duration-first principle**: add reps and extend interval length before raising power. Do not switch protocols prematurely (EC TMT-44, 45, 52, 60).

## Week 3: Recovery

Recovery week is non-negotiable. Rest-week IF should stay below 0.50 (EC TMT-55). Ride 5-6 hours maximum, all easy. Include one short opener (3 x 2 min @ 105% FTP) mid-week to maintain neuromuscular sharpness. After the block, retest 5-min peak power as your VO2max proxy (Simone, TP).

## Key Execution Notes

- **Fuel hard sessions**: low-carb during VO2max blocks impairs adaptation -- glycogen availability is essential (EC WD-54, Persp-41).
- **Allow 36-48 hours between VO2max sessions** (Santos, TP).
- **Assess each session with power + HR + RPE together** -- never a single metric in isolation (EC TMT-49).
- **Warning signs to back off**: HRV depression >7 days, performance regression at the same load, persistent low motivation, or illness (EC TMT-55).
- **Expected outcome**: look for >5% increase in 5-min peak power or lower RPE/HR at the same watts after the block (Simone, TP).
