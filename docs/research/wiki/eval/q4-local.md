Based on the provided documentation, here is a designed 3-week VO2max block tailored to a 10-hour weekly budget.

### Block Design Principles
*   **Dose:** 1-2 VO2max sessions per week [TMT-45, concepts/vo2max-training.md].
*   **Volume:** Total time at intensity should be ~15-25 minutes per session [WD-55, concepts/interval-design.md].
*   **Progression:** The "Duration-First" principle is applied, extending the length of intervals before increasing power [TMT-45, TMT-60, concepts/interval-design.md].
*   **Recovery:** VO2max sessions are spaced 36-48 hours apart [Santos (TP), concepts/vo2max-training.md].

### 3-Week VO2max Block Schedule

| Week | Session 1 (VO2max) | Session 2 (Maintenance/Endurance) | Weekly Volume |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | **Classic Long:** 5 x 3 min @ 105-120% FTP (3 min rest) [WD-55] | 1 Threshold session (e.g., 4x10 min @ FTP) + Endurance fill [TMT-45] | 10 Hours |
| **2** | **Classic Long:** 5 x 4 min @ 105-120% FTP (3-5 min rest) [WD-55] | 1 Threshold session (e.g., 3x15 min @ FTP) + Endurance fill [TMT-45] | 10 Hours |
| **3** | **Classic Long:** 4 x 5 min @ 105-120% FTP (4 min rest) [WD-55] | 1 Threshold session (e.g., 2x20 min @ FTP) + Endurance fill [TMT-45] | 10 Hours |

### Implementation Details
*   **Intensity Target:** Aim for an RPE of 9-9.5/10 [TMT-49, concepts/vo2max-training.md].
*   **Cadence:** Maintain a high cadence of 100-120 RPM to reduce muscular fatigue [WD-55, concepts/vo2max-training.md].
*   **Endurance Fill:** All other riding should be at a genuinely easy pace (IF 0.50-0.65, RPE 1-3/10) [TMT-69, concepts/training-periodization.md].
*   **Fueling:** Ensure high glycogen availability; low-carb during VO2max blocks impairs adaptation [WD-54, Persp-41, concepts/vo2max-training.md].

### Post-Block Requirement
Following this 3-week block, you must include a **rest week of 7+ days** of easy riding (IF < 0.50) to allow for adaptation [TMT-55, concepts/vo2max-training.md].
