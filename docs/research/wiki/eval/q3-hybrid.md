# Q3: Hour-by-Hour Fueling for 200km / 3000m Climbing (78kg, FTP 280W)

## Ride Demand Estimate

At 78kg with 280W FTP, a 200km ride with 3000m climbing will likely take 7-9 hours depending on terrain profile and pacing. Expected average power on climbs: 200-230W (70-82% FTP). Flat/descending: 150-180W. Estimated total energy expenditure: **4,500-5,500 kJ** [E].

At this duration, your primary performance limiter is glycogen depletion and gut tolerance, not raw power [R].

## Pre-Ride (2-3 hours before)

- Consume 2-3g carbohydrate per kg body weight: roughly **150-230g carbs** (e.g., oatmeal, banana, toast with jam, juice).
- Avoid high fat/fiber to minimize GI distress.
- Sip 500ml water with electrolytes [E].

## Hour-by-Hour Fueling Plan

**Hour 1 (easy start, settle in):**
- 60g carbs/hr. Begin eating within 15-20 minutes. Solid foods fine here (rice cakes, bars).
- 500-750ml fluid with sodium (~500mg Na) [R].

**Hours 2-3 (building intensity, climbs begin):**
- Ramp to 80-90g carbs/hr. Mix solids and gels. The gut needs training for rates above 60g/hr -- use glucose:fructose 2:1 or 1:0.8 ratio to maximize absorption via dual transport [R].
- 500-750ml/hr fluid. Increase sodium in heat (750-1000mg/hr) [E].

**Hours 4-5 (deep climbing, mid-ride):**
- Maintain 80-90g carbs/hr. Shift toward faster-absorbing sources (gels, drink mix) as intensity rises on climbs and gut blood flow decreases.
- This is the critical window -- riders who under-fuel here bonk at hour 6-7 [E].

**Hours 6-7 (fatigue setting in):**
- Continue 80-90g carbs/hr. If solid food becomes unappealing, switch entirely to gels and concentrated drink mix.
- Add caffeine (100-200mg via caffeinated gel) if not already using -- research supports ergogenic benefit in the back half of ultra-endurance efforts [R].

**Hours 8-9 (final push):**
- 60-80g carbs/hr. Absorption efficiency drops with fatigue, but keep fueling -- even if you are 30 minutes from finishing, consumed carbs take 15-20 minutes to reach working muscle.
- Small sips of cola can help (sugar + caffeine + palatability) [E].

## Totals Target

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Total carbs | 550-700g over the ride |
| Fluid | 500-750ml/hr (~5-6L total) |
| Sodium | 500-1000mg/hr |
| Caffeine | 200-400mg total (back half) |

## Key Principles

- **Start early, stay consistent.** The biggest mistake is under-fueling hours 1-3 when you feel fine.
- **Train your gut.** Practice 80-90g/hr in training rides before race day [R].
- **Dual-transport carbs** (glucose + fructose) are essential above 60g/hr [R].
- **Adjust for heat.** Add 250ml/hr fluid and extra sodium in warm conditions.
