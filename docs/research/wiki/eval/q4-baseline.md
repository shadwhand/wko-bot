# 3-Week VO2max Block (10 h/week)

## Assumptions
- FTP is established and current. All intensities reference % of FTP.
- Athlete has a solid aerobic base and can tolerate high-intensity work.

## Weekly Structure (10 hours)

| Day | Session | Duration |
|-----|---------|----------|
| Mon | Rest or easy spin | 0-0:30 |
| Tue | VO2max Intervals | 1:15 |
| Wed | Endurance Z2 | 1:30 |
| Thu | VO2max Intervals | 1:15 |
| Fri | Rest | 0 |
| Sat | Long Endurance + Tempo | 3:00-3:30 |
| Sun | Endurance Z2 | 2:00 |

## Key Workouts

### VO2max Session A -- Classic 4x4
- 15 min warm-up building to 90% FTP
- **4 x 4 min at 106-120% FTP**, 3 min recovery at 50% FTP
- Target cadence: 95-105 rpm
- 10 min cool-down
- Total: ~1:15

### VO2max Session B -- Short-Short 30/15s
- 15 min warm-up
- **3 sets of 10 x (30 sec at 120-130% FTP / 15 sec at 50% FTP)**
- 5 min between sets at 55% FTP
- 10 min cool-down
- Total: ~1:15

### VO2max Session C (Week 2-3 progression) -- 5x3 Ascending
- 15 min warm-up
- **5 x 3 min at 108%, 112%, 116%, 120%, 120% FTP**, 3 min recovery
- 10 min cool-down
- Total: ~1:15

## 3-Week Progression

### Week 1 -- Loading
- Tue: Session A (4x4 min)
- Thu: Session B (30/15s, 3 sets)
- Weekend volume: 5 hours total
- TSS target: ~450-500

### Week 2 -- Overload
- Tue: Session C (5x3 ascending)
- Thu: Session B (30/15s, increase to 12 reps per set if tolerable)
- Add 1 x 3 min at 110% FTP to Saturday long ride as a "reminder" effort
- TSS target: ~500-550

### Week 3 -- Recovery/Absorption
- Reduce total volume to ~7 hours
- Tue: Session A but 3x4 min (drop one rep)
- Thu: Easy endurance only
- Weekend volume: 4 hours easy
- TSS target: ~300-350

## Execution Guidelines

1. **Heart rate validation**: HR should reach 90-95% of HRmax by the final minute of each interval. If it does not, increase power by 3-5%.
2. **Pacing**: Start the first rep conservatively (106-108% FTP) and build across the set. Blowing up on rep 3 of 4 defeats the purpose.
3. **Recovery between intervals**: Keep pedaling at 50% FTP. Full stops reduce the oxygen kinetics stimulus.
4. **Endurance rides**: Stay strictly in Z2 (56-75% FTP). Do not drift into tempo -- the purpose is recovery and aerobic volume.
5. **Key metric to track**: Time accumulated above 90% VO2max (use HR as proxy: time above 90% HRmax during intervals). Target 12-16 min per session.
