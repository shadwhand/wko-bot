# Q4: 3-Week VO2max Block (10 Hours/Week)

## Block Design Philosophy

A dedicated VO2max block concentrates high-intensity stimuli over 2-3 weeks to maximally stress central cardiovascular adaptations (stroke volume, cardiac output, O2 delivery) [R]. With 10 hours/week, you can fit 3 quality sessions per week alongside supporting aerobic volume.

## Weekly Structure

| Day | Session | Duration | Description |
|-----|---------|----------|-------------|
| Mon | Rest or easy spin | 0-0.5hr | Recovery |
| Tue | **VO2max intervals** | 1.5hr | Key session 1 |
| Wed | Endurance Z2 | 1.5hr | Aerobic support |
| Thu | **VO2max intervals** | 1.5hr | Key session 2 |
| Fri | Rest or easy spin | 0-0.5hr | Recovery |
| Sat | **VO2max intervals** | 1.5hr | Key session 3 |
| Sun | Long endurance Z2 | 2.5-3hr | Aerobic base |

**Total: ~10 hours/week** (adjust Z2 volume to hit target).

## Interval Sessions (Rotate Through These)

**Session A -- Classic 4x4min**
- Warm-up 15min, building to threshold.
- 4-5 x 4 min at 105-120% FTP (target: reach >90% HRmax by rep 2-3). Recovery: 4 min easy spinning.
- This is the most well-validated VO2max protocol in the literature [R].

**Session B -- 30/15 Billat Intervals**
- 2-3 sets of 10-13 min: 30 sec at 105-110% FTP / 15 sec at 50% FTP. 5 min rest between sets.
- Accumulates more time at VO2max than longer intervals due to the O2 kinetics "ratchet" effect [R].

**Session C -- Decreasing Intervals**
- 5 min, 4 min, 3 min, 2 min, 1 min at 105-125% FTP (intensity rises as duration drops). Recovery: equal to work duration.
- Provides variety and addresses both aerobic and anaerobic contributions [E].

## 3-Week Progression

**Week 1 (Loading):**
- 3 x VO2max sessions (A, B, C rotation). RPE should be 8-9/10 on intervals.
- Keep Z2 rides truly easy (55-65% FTP). Do not add threshold work [E].

**Week 2 (Overreach):**
- 3 x VO2max sessions. Add 1 rep to Session A (5x4 min). Add 1 set to Session B.
- Expect accumulated fatigue -- HRV may drop, legs may feel heavy. This is intentional [E].

**Week 3 (Taper + Test):**
- 2 x VO2max sessions (Mon/Wed). Reduce volume by 30-40%.
- Friday: rest. Saturday or Sunday: FTP test or race-effort to capture gains.
- Adaptation occurs during this reduced week [R].

## Key Execution Notes

- **Hit the target.** VO2max intervals must accumulate 12-20 min at >90% HRmax per session to drive adaptation. If you cannot sustain the power, drop watts but keep HR in the zone [R].
- **Fuel aggressively.** High-carb intake (8-10g/kg/day) supports the glycolytic demand of repeated VO2max work [E].
- **Sleep 8+ hours.** Central adaptations are consolidated during sleep [E].
- **Do not add extra intensity.** The Z2 rides are recovery scaffolding, not opportunities for sweet-spot [E].

## Expected Outcome

A well-executed 3-week VO2max block typically yields 3-8% improvement in 4-8 minute power and a secondary 1-3% bump in FTP within 7-14 days of completion [R][E].
