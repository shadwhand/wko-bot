The Quick View is a convienent place to accomplish many things. You can enter notes, manage equipment, overwrite data, share your data on social media and more. Using the Quick View saves you time while still providing the functionality you need.
The Quick View is the best place to:
edit your workout data: enter post workout notes, manage equipment, overwrite data if necessary
get a quick look at all data essentials: distance, time, elevation, power/speed/hr zones, etc
share your workout to social media
much more! (add more tabs by clicking ‘More’)
The Stats tab is one of the most powerful tools in the QuickView
The
Reports
view is for analyzing the data and is read only (you cannot edit in the Reports view)
