You spend hours on your bike. Here's how to make every minute count.
As part of the partnership between the
Global Triathlon Network (GTN)

As much fun as it is to ride for hours and hours with your buddies, in terms of pure training gains, you need to be a bit more strategic with how long your ride. Essentially, if you spend almost all of your bike miles in a low zone, you could be wasting your time (although those long rides for donuts are always fun).
In the latest episode of
Triathlon Training Explained,
they highlight four workouts that keep your heart rate varied throughout so you can get the maximum training benefit in a shorter period of time and bust through any
training plateaus
