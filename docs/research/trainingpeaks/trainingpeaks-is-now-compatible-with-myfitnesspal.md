Track your nutrition in TrainingPeaks using the MyFitnessPal app. Link your accounts to seamlessly sync your data and view your calories and macronutrients in TrainingPeaks.
TrainingPeaks is now compatible with MyFitnessPal, the leading platform for tracking your nutrition. After performing a one time link, you will be able to record your meals using the free MyFitnessPal iOS, Android, and desktop apps and sync the data seamlessly to your TrainingPeaks account. Your calories and macronutrients will be visible as daily and weekly summaries on the Calendar in addition to new charts on the Dashboard. There is also the option to sync your activities from TrainingPeaks to your MyFitnessPal account.

By partnering with the leading nutrition tracking app and website, TrainingPeaks provides its users with more key functionality to help them achieve their goals. “We are excited to be offering nutrition tracking through MyFitnessPal,” says Ben Pryhoda, Chief Product Officer at TrainingPeaks. “We know how important nutrition is to endurance athletes and MyFitnessPal makes it easy for our users to track and monitor their food.”
In addition to viewing daily and weekly calories, you will be able to view charts that display your calories burned along with other metrics. This will allow you to view how your diet affects your training and performance.
