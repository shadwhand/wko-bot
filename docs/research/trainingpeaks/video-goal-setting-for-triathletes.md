Not all goals are created equal. A successful athlete should set all three types of goals in the new season. This video by the School of Tri will show you how.
When it comes to having a successful 2014 seasons, setting goals is key to staying on track. However, not all goals are created equal. This video by David Glover and the School of Tri, a great new resource for triathlon training videos, breaks down the art of goal-setting…in less than six minutes.
